## ğŸ“Œ Project Overview
Managing global logistics is complex. The **DataCo Supply Chain Intelligence Suite** is an end-to-end Tableau dashboard designed to turn raw supply chain data into clear, actionable business insights. Instead of just tracking shipments, this tool digs into *why* certain routes succeed and where hidden bottlenecks impact profitability.

By analyzing global orders, inventory, and shipping profiles, this strategic command center helps decision-makers identify:
- **Efficiency Winners:** Which shipping modes and routes drive profit and speed.
- **Hidden Bottlenecks:** Underperforming areas causing delays or reducing margins.
- **Proactive Planning:** Anticipating future demand to keep inventory balanced and customers satisfied.

## ğŸš€ Key Features & Dashboards
This suite comprises five main components:

1. **End-to-End Order Fulfillment Tracking:** Monitors the lifecycle of orders to ensure timely delivery and highlight systemic delays.
2. **Profitability & Regional Heatmaps:** Visualizes which global regions, product categories, and shipping modes are the most lucrative.
3. **Inventory & Lead-Time Optimization:** Highlights discrepancies between scheduled and actual shipping days to refine inventory planning.
4. **Supply Chain Strategy & Efficiency Analysis:** Evaluates different fulfillment strategies to maximize operational ROI.
5. **Dedicated Predictive Forecasting:** Anticipates future demand and delivery risks, enabling proactive inventory and routing adjustments.
